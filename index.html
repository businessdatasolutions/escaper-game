Diamond Quest

Collect diamonds, avoid boulders, and escape!

Diamonds: 0/10
Time: 60
Use arrow keys to move
Tap to move

How to Play:

  • • Collect the required number of diamonds
  • • Avoid falling boulders
  • • Find the exit after collecting enough diamonds
  • • Use arrow keys or on-screen buttons to move
  • • On mobile, you can also swipe to move
